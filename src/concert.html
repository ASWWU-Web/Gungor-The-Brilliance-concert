Gungor and The Brilliance

Friday, April 12, 2019, 8PM in the University Church

You can get Worship AND CommUnity credits and best of all, it's free!

Gungor is a Grammy-nominated musical collective formed by husband and wife duo Michael Gungor and Lisa Gungor. The band has released multiple albums, the last one appearing in March 2019. These albums explored a broad musical soundscape and charted the couple's changing beliefs. In early 2018, they announced a shift in direction that would see Gungor return to its Christian roots and the less mainstream material move to "other projects". This is Gungor's final tour!

The Brilliance is a band made up of David Gungor and John Arndt. Their music evolved over the years from liturgical art, to peacemaking protest music, to focusing on music that inspires empathy. The duo started making music in 2010 and released a self-titled album, The Brilliance, on October 19, 2010.
